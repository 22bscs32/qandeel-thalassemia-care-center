❤️ A Success Story of Hope

Alhamdulillah, Qandeel Thalassemia Care Center Panjgur has successfully supported more than 150 thalassemia patients through specialized treatment programs in Karachi and Peshawar.

Out of these treated patients, 146 children have shown significant improvement and no longer require regular blood transfusions.

Still, around 150 patients are waiting for treatment. Your support can help us give them the same hope and a healthier future.
