Our Patient Care Process

1. Patient Registration

Families register Thalassemia patients with basic details so the center can identify their needs and provide guidance.

2. Medical Review

Patient condition is reviewed by doctors and healthcare professionals for treatment planning and follow-up care.

3. Treatment Support

Selected patients are supported for specialized treatment in Karachi and Peshawar with the help of donors.

4. Follow-up Care

After treatment, patients receive follow-up guidance, medicines, monitoring, and family counseling.
