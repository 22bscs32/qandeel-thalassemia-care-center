About Qandeel Thalassemia Care Center

Qandeel Thalassemia Care Center Panjgur, Balochistan is a humanitarian organization working Fee Sabeel Illah for the treatment, care, awareness, and prevention of Thalassemia. The center supports children from Balochistan by arranging specialized treatment in Karachi and Peshawar, along with food, accommodation, medicines, and family support.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide free treatment support, medicines, food, accommodation, and care to children affected by Thalassemia Major.

🌍 Our Vision

To build a Thalassemia-Free Balochistan through awareness, prevention, pre-marital testing, and equal access to treatment.

❤️ Fee Sabeel Illah

The entire mission runs through public donations, Zakat, Sadaqah, and sincere community support.

Founder's Journey

A father's promise became a mission of hope for hundreds of children suffering from Thalassemia across Balochistan.

Khuda Nazar Baloch

Founder & Chairman

Khuda Nazar Baloch founded Qandeel Thalassemia Care Center after his own daughter was diagnosed with Thalassemia Major. His personal experience inspired him to dedicate his life to helping children across Balochistan.

Today, the center provides specialized treatment, medicines, accommodation, food, and patient care for children through public donations and community support.

Under his leadership, 150 children are receiving treatment, and 146 children are now living transfusion-free on oral medication.

“If my daughter recovers, I will dedicate my life Fee Sabeel Illah to the children of Balochistan.”

Our Prevention Mission

🧪 Pre-Marital Testing

We encourage every individual to get Hb Electrophoresis testing before marriage to know their carrier status.

📢 Awareness Campaigns

We conduct awareness sessions in schools, colleges, villages, and communities to educate people about Thalassemia prevention.

🤝 Equal Access

We believe no child in Balochistan should be left without treatment because of poverty, cost, or distance.

Message from the Founder
