Your Donation Changes Lives

Qandeel Thalassemia Care Center Panjgur currently supports more than 300 registered Thalassemia patients across the district.

Through the generosity of donors and volunteers, the center has successfully arranged specialized treatment programs in Karachi and Peshawar for more than 150 patients.

By the grace of Allah, 146 children have shown significant improvement and no longer require regular blood transfusions.
